Everything a working writer needs

A high-end writing instrument — not another productivity app.

Designed to feel like a well-made notebook: quiet, warm, and built to make showing up every day feel good.

Daily dashboard

Today's words against your goal, your live streak, and every active project's pace — the moment you open the app.

Pace calculator

"At your current pace, you'll finish on March 14th." Set a deadline and WordForge tells you exactly how many words a day it takes.

Rich analytics

A full-year contribution grid, words-per-day charts, best-weekday heatmaps, and all-time stats that make progress impossible to ignore.

Sprint mode

15, 25, 45, or 60-minute focus sprints with a gentle chime when time's up — then log your words in a single tap.

Drafts & milestones

Track multiple projects and drafts. Cross 10k, 25k, 50k, 75k, 100k and your target — each one a small celebration.

Truly yours

No cloud, no sign-up, no telemetry. Your data is one local file you can export to CSV or JSON — or drop into iCloud yourself.

The long view

A year of writing, at a glance.

Every square is a day. Watch the gold spread across the calendar as the habit takes hold — the most motivating picture a writer can have.

  • Full-year contribution grid
  • Best-day, monthly totals & words-per-hour
  • All-time totals and your longest streak ever
